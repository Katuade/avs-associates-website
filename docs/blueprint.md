# **App Name**: AVS Associates

## Core Features:

- Information Display: Presents key information about call center services.
- About Us: about us section
- Our Services: our services
- Our Clients: our clients
- Contact Us: mobile, email and address

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) for trust and sophistication.
- Background color: Light teal (#E0F8F7), almost white.
- Accent color: Maroon  to highlight important sections.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' serif for elegance; body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for readability.
- Use clean, professional icons representing call center services and benefits.
- Clean, well-spaced layout with clear section dividers.
- Subtle animations on scrolling to showcase information.
